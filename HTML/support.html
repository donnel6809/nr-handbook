Support

By now you have probably set up your New Relic account, and you may even have data reporting already, which is awesome! If not, or if you run into any issues along the way, that's OK too. Below are some available resources for you to get help.

  1. Reach out in #new-relic-admins if you have questions about anything in this handbook.
  2. For continuous learning with New Relic, sign up for an account & some courses in the New Relic University
  3. Search your question in the New Relic Explorers Hub, New Relic Support Portal, or the New Relic Documentation portal.
  4. If you cannot find an answer with the above resources, post your question to the Explorers Hub where other New Relic users, along with New Relic staff can help.
